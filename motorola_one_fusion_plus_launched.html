Motorola One Fusion+ Launched In India

updated: 16 june 2020

Motorola One Fusion+ has been launched in india.The latest device is packed with 5000mAh battery, a pop-up selfie camera and a quad camera setup at the back having 64 megapixel main sensor.

Motorola One Fusion+ Price In India

This device is priced at 16,999 for 6GB + 128GB version.It will be available on flipkart from 24th of june 12PM

Motorola One Fusion+ Specifications

The phone comes with 128gigs of internal storage and a microSD card slot which can support cards up to 1TB.It is equiped with a snapdragon 730G SoC and Adreno 618 GPU.The device have a 6.5-inch full-HD+ display (notch-less) with resolution 1,080 * 2,340 (19.5:9 aspect ratio) and the whole system runs on stock Android 10

The pop-up camera is of 16MP withf/2.2 aperture. The rear quad camera module is at the top left corner of the device featuring a 64MP main camera (f/1.8 aperture), a 8MP ultra-wide angle camera (f/2.2 aperture), a 5MP macro lens (2.4 aperture), and a 2-MP depth sensor(f/2.4 aperture) The phone is packed with 5000mAH battery and 15W fast charging support. It is available in two color variants Twilight Blue and Moonlight White the both variants have dimensions 162.9*76.9*9.6mm and weighs 210gm.
